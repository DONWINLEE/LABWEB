Postdoc positions

Two postdoctoral Research Fellow positions are available in Yi Group@NTU. One position is in the area of coastal underground seawater intrusion prevention and freshwater resource utilization, and the other one focuses on concrete fracturing for demolition. The successful candidates are expected to commerce duty in Q4 2026.

Requirements:

  • Highly self-motivated. Ability to work independently and as part of a team;
  • Research experience in laboratory experiment and numerical modelling on relevant topics;
  • PhD degree from reputable universities/institutions;
  • Good publication record in decent journals;
  • Good English writing and communication skills.

If you are interested, please send your CV to PI (yiyaolin@ntu.edu.sg).

PhD applicants and visiting PhD students in these areas are also welcome!
