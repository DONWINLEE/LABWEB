Yi Group@NTU on Sustainable Geotechnics

Global sustainable development faces challenges including climate change, sea level rise, greenhouse gas emission, high consumption of non-renewable resource and energy, waste disposal, and environmental pollution. Geotechnical engineering is one of the most resource intensive disciplines, and the research group led by Prof. Yaolin Yi at Nanyang Technological University (Yi Group@NTU) attempts to use interdisciplinary approaches to improve the sustainability in geotechnical engineering.
